Hi! I earned my Computer Science degree (BS) at the University of California, Davis. I'm passionate about all things software and am always on the lookout to grow and push the boundaries of what I know! I'm the person staying up until 2am fixing the space margins on my site.

My Design Goals

01
Scalability

While great products often start from small ideas, true success lies in their ability to expand. I strive to develop end-products that not only meet initial design goals but also robustly scale to meet increased demands.
02
Reliability

A product's success depends on its ability to consistently meet user expectations, which in turn builds trust. I ensure that products not only scale with demand but also maintain a dependable experience, even in the face of technical challenges.
03
Practical & Innovative

Delivering real-world solutions requires addressing real-world constraints, from production costs to customer needs. I strive to balance creativity with practicality, ensuring that solutions are not only cutting-edge but also equally sustainable and impactful.
